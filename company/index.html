Friendwell Group of Companies specializes in acquisition and managing income- producing real estate, such as shopping centers, office buildings & hotels Friendwell operate business very similar to a REIT, a company pays virtually all of its taxable income to its shareholders every year. So that Company's assets are primarily composed of real estate held for the long term. Company's income is mainly derived from real estate. Company pays out all of its taxable income to shareholders. Friendwell’s acquisition and management focus is on shopping centers, office buildings & hotels in the North Eastern United States. We finance our transactions through our capital partners, investment bankers & capital generated from our income.

That's why at Friendwell, we are committed to provide real estate solutions that maximize our business performance and enhance investment profitability. Investor Info

Our investment strategy is focused primarily on commercial real estate properties in North East U.S.—From Boston along I-95 to Washington, D.C. Our target markets are generally considered the “power generators” of US economy, with highly educated work forces and high concentrations of colleges, universities and diversified industries. It is our belief that Markets with these characteristics are more resilient to the ups and downs of the real estate cycle and offer greater and more consistent strength over the long run. In evaluation prospective investments, Friendwell looks for the following basic characteristic:
Strong long-term demand fundamentals:
Markets that have broad, diverse economies and are dominated by industries with strong, long-term growth prospects.
Opportunities to capitalize on market inefficiencies:
Assets that possess strong fundamentals, but that may be impaired or undervalued due to excess leverage, temporary market illiquidity or weak ownership.
Opportunities to apply operating expertise:
Friendwell also looks for opportunities where it can add value to assets through its operating skills and expertise.
